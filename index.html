    Ingredients

    • Cooking Oil
    • Onions and Peppers
    • Raw Liver
    • Salt and Spices
    • Hearty Beef Soup Pack

    Recipe

    1. "Step 1":[Chop your onion and peppers ,and fry them in hot cooking oil]
    2. "Step 2":[Add spices and salt and stir until mixed nicely,then pour your liver cut into quarter squares]
    3. "Step 3":[Let it fry in a medium heat and cover the pot for a few minutes]
    4. "Step 4":[Stir again and open the pot to fry the liver until you see its cooked perfectly]
    5. "Step 5":[Start to prepare your Hearty beef soup thickner with water ,poor the liver and let it simmer for a few minutes]
    6. "Step 6":[Its Ready!You may enjoy it with regular bread or steamed bread. Enjoy!]
